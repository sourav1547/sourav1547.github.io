Sourav Das

I am a final year PhD student in Computer Science at UIUC, working with Prof. Ling Ren on Security, Applied Cryptography and Distributed Computing.

I did my undergraduate studies from IIT Delhi, where I was advised by Prof. Vinay Ribeiro. After finishing my bachelors, I also had the fortune to visit National University of Singapore and IIT Bombay as a Research Assistant.

I am highly honored to receive the 2022 ChainLink Labs Ph.D. fellowship. I am also a finalist of the 2022 Meta (Facebook) Ph.D. fellowship.

I am on the job market in the 2024-25 cycle!

Research Interests

I'm broadly interested in Security, Applied Cryptography, and Distributed Computing. I have also worked on Scalability of Smart Contracts for permissionless blockchains.
